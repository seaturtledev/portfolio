    About Me

    I'm an engineer and builder who keeps gravitating toward problems where the technical challenge and the human stakes are equally high. The most interesting work, to me, sits exactly where those two overlap.

    My Approach

    Over the past five years that's meant writing software that helps kids learn arithmetic, prototyping a wearable that could detect drowning, bringing music into senior care homes, and partnering with government to turn ideas into policy and programs that actually reach people.

    The Goal

    Whatever the medium — code, hardware, research, or partnership — the thread is the same: I like shipping things that leave the world a little more capable than I found it, and I like working with people who feel the same way.
